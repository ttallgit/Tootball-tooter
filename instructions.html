HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

↑ : Run Forward

← → ↑ ↓ : Move Crosshair

SPACE (hold) : Charge Power

SPACE (release) : Shoot

PLACEMENT

Use arrow keys to aim the crosshair within the goal.

The crosshair has inertia — it keeps moving after you release.

Aim for corners or opposite side of the keeper's dive.

POWER

Hold SPACE to charge — the power bar oscillates up and down.

Release at the right moment for your desired power level.

More power = faster shot (harder for keeper) but less accurate.

Less power = more accurate but keeper has more time to save.

THE KEEPER

The keeper dives LEFT, CENTER, or RIGHT — you don't know which!

Shots close to the keeper's dive are saved.

Shots to the opposite side or tight corners beat the keeper.

Top and bottom corners are harder for the keeper to reach.

KEEPER LEARNING

The keeper watches where you aim and learns your tendencies.

Go to the same spot too many times and the keeper will predict it.

Mix up your placement to keep the keeper guessing!
