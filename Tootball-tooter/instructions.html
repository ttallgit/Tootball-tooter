HOW TO PLAY

CONTROLS

↑ : Run Forward

← → ↓ : Choose Shot Direction

SPACE : Shoot

THE ARROW

A flashing arrow shows which way the goalkeeper will dive.

You must shoot in the OPPOSITE direction to score!

TIMING

Press direction + SPACE at the EXACT same time (within 15ms).

You have 500ms after "SHOOT!" to press the buttons.

HARD MODE

Arrow can also point UP (center shot).

Press ↓ + SPACE to shoot the opposite way!

The goalkeeper stays center when arrow points up.

EXAMPLES

← Shoot → + SPACE = GOAL!
→ Shoot ← + SPACE = GOAL!
↑ Shoot ↓ + SPACE = GOAL!
